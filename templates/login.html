Project SPLIT

DAR Negros Island Region

Supporting parcelization and individual titling under DAR Project SPLIT.

Project SPLIT is a DAR initiative supported by the World Bank to help convert collective CLOAs into individual land titles, improving land tenure security for agrarian reform beneficiaries nationwide.

DAR Mission A just, safe, and equitable society

DAR Vision Fair land distribution with empowered communities

A rural Philippines where farmers enjoy secure ownership, sustainable livelihoods, and active participation in national development.
